A fully offline migraine tracker for Android. Track migraine attacks, acute medications, triggers, symptoms, and life changes. Built-in Medication Overuse Headache (MOH) risk gauges with per-category thresholds and customizable push notifications for preventative medication reminders and daily check-ins.

All data stays on your device — no accounts, no cloud, no tracking. Dark mode optimized for light-sensitive users. Analytics show attack frequency, severity trends, trigger correlations, and symptom patterns over time.
